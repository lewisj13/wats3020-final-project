Features

Fastest shooting 1D

Capable of up to 14 fps full-resolution RAW or JPEG, and up to 16 fps in Live View mode with new Dual DIGIC 6+ Image Processors.

Learn More

Higher Maximum Burst

Maximum burst rate of up to 170 RAWs in continuous shooting at up to 16 fps, and 4K movies using CFast™ cards in the new CFast 2.0™ slot.

Learn More

Experience less Noise

Experience less noise in higher ISO images via a new 20.2 Megapixel full-frame CMOS sensor, with an ISO range of 100–51200.

Learn More

AF Performance

Improved AF performance through 61-point High Density Reticular AF II system with 41 cross-type points will exceed expectations.
